Oman - a beautiful country with very friendly people

Important towns

Muscat

Muscat (Arabic: مَسْقَط,) is the capital and most populated city in Oman. It is the seat of the Governorate of Muscat. The total population of Muscat Governorate was 1.72 million in September 2022. Important things to visit:

  • Sultan Quaboos Grand Mosque
  • Royal Opera House
  • Muttrah Souq
  • Qurum beach

Nizwa

Nizwa (Arabic: نِزْوَى, romanized: Nizwá) was the former capital of Oman and is still one of the most important cities of the country. Nizwa is about 140 km (1.5 hours) from Muscat. The population is estimated at around 83.544 people. Important things to visit:

  • Nizwa Fort
  • Nizwa Souk
  • Sultan Quaboos Mosque

Salala

Salala (Arabic: صَلَالَة, romanized: Ṣalālah) is the capital and largest city of the southern Omani governorate of Dhofar. As of the census of 2020, it had a population of 331,949 and is the third largest city in Oman. Important things to visit:

  • Souk al Husn
  • Sultan Quaboos Mosque
  • Al Mughsaid Beach
